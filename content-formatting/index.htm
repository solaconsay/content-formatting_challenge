Introduction to HTML Formatting

Block level vs Inline elements

The <p> element is a block level element. It occupies the entire horizontal space of its parent element, and vertical space equal to the height of its contents, thereby creating a "block".

On the other hand, the <strong> element is an inline element. Inline elements display in a line. They do not force the text after them to a new line.
